HI, WE’RE ASSEMBLY.

We’re a small, collaborative roastery, located in Brooklyn, Wellington, bringing together ethical, organic beans and good people to make beautiful, unique coffee.

We love coffee. It charges our batteries. It gives us a reason to switch on in the morning and motor on late into the night – all in pursuit of the perfect brew.
